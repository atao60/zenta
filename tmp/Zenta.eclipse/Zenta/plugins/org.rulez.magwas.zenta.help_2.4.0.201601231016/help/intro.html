Introduction

Zenta is a free, open source, cross-platform tool to create models.

Zenta is targeted toward all levels of Enterprise Architects and Enterprise Modellers. It is intended to provide a low cost to entry (i.e. free) solution to users who may be making their first steps in modeling and who are looking for a cross-platform modelling tool for their company or institution. Zenta fulfils the needs of most Enterprise Architects and associated stakeholders.

Zenta is forked from Archi, with the main goal to becoma a metamodel-driven modeling tool

The development of Archi has been funded by JISC and has been developed by Phillip Beauvoir for JISC CETIS.
