Templates

Zenta supports the use of re-usable models, or templates. A template is a model that has been saved in an archive file with the extension "*.architemplate". Further information describing the template and optional thumb-nail images of the model’s Views is also contained in the template file. Templates are convenient starting points for creating new models.

To see a screen-cast demonstration of Templates go to this web-site: http://zenta.magwas.rulez.org/movies/templates/templates.html
